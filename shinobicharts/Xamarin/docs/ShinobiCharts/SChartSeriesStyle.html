SChartSeriesStyle Class

SChartSeriesStyle is the base class for all series styles in ShinobiCharts.

Syntax

[Foundation.Register("SChartSeriesStyle", true)]
public abstract class SChartSeriesStyle : Foundation.NSObject

Remarks

Series style objects manage the appearance of series on a chart. SChartSeriesStyle allows you to modify the representation of the series in the chart legend. In order to tweak properties on a series style object which are specific to that kind of series, you should look at the documentation for the concrete series styles which are available. These are:
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartBarSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of bar series.
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartColumnSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of column series.
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartCandlestickSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of candlestick series.
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartDonutSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of donut series.
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartLineSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of line series.
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartBandSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of band series.
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartOHLCSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of Open-High-Low-Closed (OHLC) series.
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartPieSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of pie series.
  • ShinobiCharts.SChartScatterSeriesStyle - manages the appearance of scatter series.
